Welcome to SA Design & Technology

Learn more about us



SA DesTech is the nickname for this small IT group which consists of a freelance programmer and technician also a freelance graphic designer. Both of the founder are students from UiTM Melaka, Jasin campus who currently studying in Bachelor of Computer Science (Honour). SA DesTech provides services on designing, fixing laptop and web development. There will also be training in basic Photoshop and basic programming in future.
